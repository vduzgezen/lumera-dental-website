About Oria

We build one thing exceptionally well: zirconia crowns. When other restorations reach the efficiency and reliability of The Crown, we’ll add them. Never before.

Our Story

We started as a boutique lab for personalized restorations, a small team obsessed with detail and dependable seat times. As a private lab we served a network of 50+ clinics in the Northeast (52 at launch) and refined a single workflow end to end. Today we’re opening to the public with that same craft and focus.

How We Work

Scans only (STL/PLY), no hard impressions. Our globally distributed design team keeps projects moving overnight and answers questions quickly. Production is Made in USA for consistency and precision. We promise delivery in 7 business days.

Our design bench has generational experience with top quality dentists, pairing clinical nuance with modern automation. The result is a fast, clear, repeatable workflow.

Roadmap

As additional restorations meet our Crown-level efficiency and outcomes, we will add them methodically. Today The Crown is $60 all-in. Future products will launch with a single, simple price and the same clarity.
